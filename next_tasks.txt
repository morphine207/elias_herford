# Next Tasks & Experience Flow


## 1. Content Summary Page (after Preferences)
- Present a summary of the selected content once the learner sets their preferences.
- Include:
  - Main ideas and highlights formatted according to the chosen learning style (visual / audio / text).
  - Persistent modules: feedback box, virtual assistant panel, and tech news board (state-of-the-art updates, with the option to convert items into proposals or new content).

## 2. Initial Quiz & Evaluation Flow
1. After the summary, route the learner to an initial quiz covering the key points.
2. On completion:
   - **Pass**
     - Show a content-specific leaderboard displaying completion time, average score, and peer ranking.
     - Provide optional internal interaction tools (messaging, team chat, discussion threads).
     - Display a community box where colleagues can share, comment on, or rate user-generated assets (videos, podcasts, tool suggestions).
     - Redirect to a content overview page listing all courses, current progress, and options to start another course.
   - **Fail**

## 3. Persistent UI Enhancements (all pages)
- Dark / light mode toggle for accessibility.
- Language switcher (German / English) in the top-right corner.
